# Introducing OmniPost: Publish Once, Reach Every Platform

*Posted on March 30, 2026 | 6 min read*

---

If you manage content across multiple social platforms, you already know the drill. You write a post for LinkedIn, then rewrite it for Twitter, resize the image for Instagram, adjust the tone for Facebook, and by the time you're done, an hour has evaporated. Multiply that by five posts a week, and you're spending more time reformatting than creating.

We built OmniPost to end that cycle.

## What is OmniPost?

OmniPost is an AI-powered content publishing platform that lets you write once and publish everywhere. Connect your accounts on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter, compose your content in a single editor, and let OmniPost adapt it to each platform automatically -- adjusting character limits, tone, formatting, and image dimensions so every post looks native to where it lands.

But OmniPost is more than a cross-poster. It's a full publishing workflow: create, adapt, schedule, publish, and measure -- all from one dashboard.

## Key Features

### 1. AI Content Adaptation

Write your core message once. OmniPost's AI engine rewrites it for each platform, respecting character limits, hashtag conventions, and audience expectations. A long-form LinkedIn insight becomes a punchy tweet. A professional update becomes a casual Instagram caption. You review, tweak if needed, and publish.

### 2. Smart Scheduling

OmniPost analyzes your audience engagement patterns and suggests optimal posting times for each platform. Set it and forget it, or fine-tune the schedule yourself. Either way, your content goes live when your audience is most active -- not when you happen to be at your desk.

### 3. AI Image Generation

Need a visual for your post? Describe what you want, and OmniPost generates platform-optimized images in the right dimensions. No more juggling Canva templates or manually resizing PNGs. The AI handles composition, and you keep creative control.

### 4. Unified Analytics

See how your content performs across every platform in one view. Track engagement, reach, and growth without switching between four different analytics dashboards. Spot what's working, double down on it, and let the data guide your content strategy.

## Who is OmniPost For?

### Content Creators

You're a one-person operation producing content across multiple channels. Every minute counts. OmniPost gives you back the hours you currently spend copy-pasting and reformatting, so you can focus on what you do best: creating.

### Marketing Teams

Your team coordinates campaigns across platforms, and version control is a nightmare. OmniPost centralizes content creation and scheduling so everyone works from the same source of truth. Built-in CRM features help you track leads generated from your content efforts.

### Small Business Owners and Social Media Managers

You're managing social presence for one or more brands, and your tool stack is getting expensive and fragmented. OmniPost consolidates publishing, scheduling, analytics, and lead management into a single platform starting at $0/month.

## What Makes OmniPost Different

**AI-native from day one.** OmniPost wasn't built as a scheduler with AI bolted on. The AI engine is central to the product -- it adapts content, generates images, suggests timing, and summarizes performance. Every feature is designed around the assumption that AI should handle the repetitive work.

**Open source.** OmniPost is MIT-licensed and open source. You can inspect the code, contribute features, self-host if you prefer, or simply trust that there's no black box behind the product. We believe publishing tools should be transparent.

**Built-in CRM.** Most publishing tools stop at "post published." OmniPost includes lead scoring, engagement tracking, and nurture sequences so you can connect your content efforts directly to business outcomes. No need for a separate CRM integration just to see if your content is driving results.

**Built on modern foundations.** OmniPost runs on Next.js 16, React 19, and TypeScript with a PostgreSQL backend. It's fast, type-safe, and built to scale. For the developers in the room: the entire stack is something you'd want to contribute to, not work around.
